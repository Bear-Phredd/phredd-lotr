    Building Level Edit

    Rank Points Edit

    First of all, Rank Points may be misleading. Rank Points add to your City Score, not your Rank. Your Rank is based on your position in your World, which is in turn based on your City Score. Do not expect Rank Points to change your ranking by their quoted numbers!

    The Rank Points for each building show their cumulative total and not their points for that particular level. For example, a level 10 Town Hall pays 75 Rank Points to bring its total to 300 (not 300 points above level 9). To find Rank Points earned for a particular level, subtract the current level from the next level's points.

    Example: Town Hall level 10 = 300 RP, Town Hall level 9 = 225RP, therefore you will gain 75RP for leveling your Town Hall from 9 to 10.

    Upgrading Edit

    This article is a stub. You can help Lord of Ultima Wiki by expanding it.

    Downgrading Edit

    You can reduce a buildings level by one by selecting it and then clicking the button at the bottom of the building information panel labelled Downgrade to level N-1, where N is the buildings current level.

    The Demolish button will completely remove the building. For a level one building the Downgrade button is replaced by a Destroy this building button (in addition to the Demolish button [why have both?]).

    Downgrading takes half the time and refunds half of the resources of the corresponding upgrade. For example, upgrading a level two Shipyard costs 200w, 400s and takes 24 minutes. Downgrading a level three Shipyard will repay 100w, 200s and take 12 minutes.

    Action Resource Cost Wood Resource Cost Stone Execution Time
    Upgrade of lvl 2 Shipyard 200 400 24 minutes
    Wood acquired Stone acquired
    Downgrade of lvl 3 Shipyard 100 200 12 minutes

    List of Buildings Edit

    Town Hall Edit

    Provides basic storage and wood production. Also determines the maximum number of buildings in a city. Only one can be built in each city. Cannot be totally demolished or destroyed.

    Main article: Town Hall

    Woodcutter's Hut Edit

    Produces wood. Any bordering woods, Cottages and a maximum of one Sawmill will increase productivity. There was an interim time when production was 16, 32, 50, 70, 90, 115, 145, 170, 205 and 250.

    Main article: Woodcutter's Hut

    Cottage Edit

    Increases the construction speed of all build orders in a city. Also increases productivity of bordering resource buildings.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 1

    Main article: Cottage

    Warehouse Edit

    Increases the storage capacity for all resources. Enhanced by any bordering Sawmill, Stonemason, Mill or Foundry.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 1

    Main article: Warehouse

    Quarry Edit

    Produces Stone. Productivity can be increased by bordering Rocks, Cottages and a maximum of one Stonemason. There was an interim time when production was 16, 32, 50, 70, 90, 115, 145, 170, 205 and 250.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 2

    Main article: Quarry

    Hideout Edit

    Allows safe storage of resources, preventing them from being reported by Scouts or plundered. Its capacity can be increased if built beside woods.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 2

    Main article: Hideout

    Farm Edit

    Produces food. Any bordering grassland, lakes, Cottages and Mills will increase productivity.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 3

    Main article: Farm

    Iron Mine Edit

    Produces iron. Any bordering ore deposits, Cottages and a maximum of one Foundry will increase productivity. There was an interim time when production was 16, 32, 50, 70, 90, 115, 145, 170, 205 and 250.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 4

    Main article: Iron Mine

    Townhouse Edit

    Enables taxation. Income from tax can be increased by connecting Marketplaces and Harbors.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 5

    Main article: Townhouse

    Marketplace Edit

    Allows transport of resources between cities and enables trade with other players by land. Also increases the tax income of connected buildings.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 5

    Main article: Marketplace

    Harbor Edit

    Allows for the transport of resources between cities and enables you to trade with other players by sea. Also increases the tax income of connected Townhouses.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 10

    Main article: Harbor

    Sawmill Edit

    Increases the efficiency of bordering Woodcutter's Huts and wood storage capacity of any bordering Storage.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 6

    Main article: Sawmill

    Stonemason Edit

    Increases the efficiency of bordering Quarries and stone storage capacity of any bordering Storage.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 7

    Main article: Stonemason

    Mill Edit

    Increases the efficiency of bordering Farms and food storage capacity of any bordering Storage.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 8

    Main article: Mill

    Foundry Edit

    Increases the efficiency of bordering Ore Mines and iron storage capacity of bordering Storage.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 9

    Main article: Foundry

    City Wall Edit

    Increases the defense of all units, except naval units, against all attacks. Cannot be totally demolished or destroyed.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 2

    Main article: City Wall

    City Guard House Edit

    Allows recruitment of City Guards and, if upgraded, increases their recruitment speed. Placing a Barracks beside a City Guard House will NOT increase recruitment speed.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 3

    Main article: City Guard House

    Lookout Tower Edit

    Enables incoming attacks to be seen sooner. (Time without Lookout Tower: 1 Hour). Increases the Defensive Value of a limited amount of Scouts by 100%. If there are not enough Scouts in the city, 2 city guards can take each slot and gain the same bonus. Lookout Towers don't count towards a city's building number limit.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 2

    Main article: Lookout Tower

    Ranger Tower Edit

    Increases the Defensive Value of a limited number of Rangers by 100%. If there are not enough Rangers in the city, City Guards can take their place and gain the same bonus. These towers don't count towards a city's building number limit.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 3

    Main article: Ranger Tower

    Guardian Tower Edit

    Increases the Defensive Value of a limited number of Guardians by 100%. If there are not enough Guardians in the city, City Guards can take their place and gain the same bonus. These towers don't count towards a city's building number limit.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 5

    Main article: Guardian Tower

    Barracks Edit

    Increases the maximum army size of a city. Also increases the recruitment speed of all bordering Training Grounds, Stables, Moonglow Towers, Trinsic Temples, Workshops and Shipyards.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 4

    Main article: Barracks

    Training Ground Edit

    Allows recruitment of infantry units and, if upgraded, increases their recruitment speed. Placing a Barracks beside a Training Ground will also increase recruitment speed.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 4

    Main article: Training Ground

    Pitfall TrapEdit

    Pitfall Traps neutralize the troop strength of a limited number of enemy infantry units. Neutralized attackers will not deal damage to defenders. A maximum of 50% of infantry attackers will be affected. Pitfall Traps can only be built on the outer ring of the City Wall, and dont count towards a citys building number limit.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 5

    Main article: Pitfall Trap

    StableEdit

    Allows recruitment of cavalry units and, if upgraded, increases their recruitment speed. Placing a Barracks beside a Stable will also increase recruitment speed.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 6

    Main article: Stable

    Guardian TowerEdit

    Increases the Defensive Value of a limited number of Guardians by 100%. If there are not enough Guardians in the city, City Guards can take their place and gain the same bonus. These towers dont count towards a citys building number limit.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 6

    Main article: Guardian Tower

    Moonglow TowerEdit

    Allows recruitment of caster units (Mages, Warlocks) and, if upgraded, increases their recruitment speed. Also enables purification of resources for research, vital when recruiting Barons. Placing a Barracks beside a Moonglow Tower will also increase recruitment speed.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 7

    Main article: Moonglow Tower

    BarricadeEdit

    Barricades neutralize the troop strength of a limited number of enemy cavalry units. Neutralized attackers will not deal damage to defenders. A maximum of 50% of cavalry attackers will be affected. Barricades can only be built on the outer ring of the City Wall, and dont count towards a citys building number limit.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 7

    Main article: Barricade

    CastleEdit

    Construction of the Castle allows a lord to plunder cities (castled or not) and assault or siege castled cities. Each level of the Castle increases your maximum army size and expands your command queue by one. Only one Castle can be built in each city. Cannot be totally demolished or destroyed. Once built a Castle cannot be removed. Every 4th Title starting with Marquess is available free with an extra castle. Note: Building a Castle allows the castled city to be assaulted, sieged and/or captured.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 8

    Main article: Castle

    Trinsic TempleEdit

    Allows recruitment of blessed units and Barons and, if upgraded, increases their recruitment speed. Placing a Barracks beside a Trinsic Temple will also increase recruitment speed.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 8

    Main article: Trinsic Temple


    Templar TowerEdit

    Increases the Defensive Value of a limited number of Templars by 100%. If there are not enough Templars in the city, City Guards can take their place and gain the same bonus. These towers dont count towards a citys building number limit.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 8

    Main article: Templar Tower

    Arcane TrapEdit

    Arcane Traps neutralize the troop strength of a limited number of enemy magic user units. Neutralized attackers will not deal damage to defenders. A maximum of 50% of magic using attackers will be affected. Arcane Traps can only be built on the outer ring of the City Wall, and dont count towards a citys building number limit.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 8

    Main article: Arcane Trap

    Ballista TowerEdit

    Increases the Defensive Value of a limited number of Ballistae by 100%. If there are not enough Ballistae in the city, 10 City Guards can take each allotted slot and gain the same bonus. These towers dont count towards a citys building number limit.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 9

    Main article: Ballista Tower

    WorkshopEdit

    Allows construction of siege engines and, if upgraded, increases their construction speed. Placing a Barracks beside a Workshop will also increase recruitment speed.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 9

    Main article: Workshop

    ShipyardEdit

    Enables recruitment of naval units and, if upgraded, increases their recruitment speed. Placing a Barracks nearby further increases recruitment speed.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 10

    Main article: Shipyard

    Camouflage TrapEdit

    Camouflage Trap neutralize the troop strength of a limited number of enemy artillery units. Neutralized attackers will not deal damage to defenders, fortifications and buildings. A maximum of 50% of artillery units will be affected. Camouflage Traps can only be built on the outer ring of the City Wall, and dont count towards a citys building number limit.

    Requires: Town Hall Level 10

    Main article: Camouflage Trap
